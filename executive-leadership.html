This portfolio is not a generic “entrepreneurship” claim. It is a record of founder-level execution across grant-funded medical-device R&D, regulatory-scientific consulting, laboratory and engineering infrastructure, investor/partner materials, and early-stage venture operations.

GAMA Therapeutics

Photomedicine and infection-control venture. CSO / co-founder / board voting member. Pitch materials documented a Mansfield BioIncubator BSL-2 lab, a 5 full-time / 4 part-time operating team, $1.8M seed/grant funding, and $20M+ in grant opportunities under review or in progress. UV Light Care

Healthcare safety venture focused on UV/light-based infection control. Built M2D2-facing materials around catheter-associated infection burden, CLABSI/MRSA problem framing, germicidal UV catheter-lumen disinfection, portability, and low-training use. Translated photobiology, biofilm science, and optical-test-bed concepts into an early device-development pathway.

Gliese 623B

Research consultancy and venture vehicle supporting sponsored research, UMass Lowell M2D2 collaboration, biofilm methods, optics, contract-research proposals, and stakeholder communication. Evidence includes catheter-segment biofilm-apparatus planning, continuous-flow reactor concepts, peristaltic-pump / bubble-trap / luer-component selection, UV-transparent materials review, and cost-saving laboratory redesign.

GROW-RI

Agritech / controlled-environment agriculture venture tied to UV/visible-light crop sanitation, photobiomodulation, fungal/pathogen reduction, software-controlled LED growth and pest-control panels, facility automation, crop monitoring, data analytics, and robotic-harvesting concepts. Public coverage by VerticalFarmDaily identified Tony as CSO and described GROW-RI’s GENESIS automation systems for greenhouses and indoor farms. Internal evidence materials frame a $3M funding plan for a Warwick, Rhode Island plant-pathology lab, prototyping, staffing, market research, licensing, and IP strategy.

BioCellR8

Scientific, regulatory, and commercialization consulting platform spanning nutraceuticals, cosmetics, cannabis/CBD claims risk, export frameworks, commercialization plans, joint-venture documentation, BSL-2 compliance frameworks, and emerging-client technical strategy. Evidence includes MoCRA / FD&C / FDA / FTC claim-risk analysis, ISO 22716 quality-system planning, safety substantiation, vendor/SOP review, and JV/IP structure review.
