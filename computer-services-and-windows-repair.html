Technical Advocacy for Real-World Computer Problems

I help individuals, households, sole proprietors, teachers, researchers, and small organizations make their computers and digital operations less chaotic and more useful. My work combines careful Windows troubleshooting, PowerShell-assisted repair, file organization, website development, e-commerce setup, online marketing workflows, AI-enabled productivity, and plain-English documentation.

This is not a rushed “click-and-hope” repair model. I work like a technical advocate: identify the actual problem, protect your data, document what changed, and leave you with a system that is easier to understand after I am gone.

Best fit: messy Windows machines, overloaded storage, confusing file trees, failed migrations, broken workflows, website deployment headaches, customer database cleanup, Stripe or Netlify setup, Google/Office automation, AI tool setup, and “I know this computer or business process can work better but I do not know where to begin” situations.
